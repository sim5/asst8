Sky War

  • High Concept: A shooter game in the sky.
  • Player Experience Goals: Concentration to shoot the enemy while avoiding the enemy bullets.
  • Genre: Shooter.
  • Game play: The player moves with mouse, shoots with left click, when the power gets to 100%, the player can press 'A' to use the super power. Smiley face doubles the shooting speed.
  • Prototype goal: Shooting, moving, dodging.
  • Features: Super power is available every 5 kills. Smiley face doubles the shooting speed.
  • Setting/Premise: It is World War III and you are the only pilot from your nation who survived in the battle. You are in the middle of the ocean.
  • Story: It is World War III and you are the only pilot from your nation who survived in the battle. You are in the middle of the ocean. You have to survive for 50 seconds until your allies rescue you.
  • Target Audience: Any age group who can control with mouse and keyboard.
  • Play time: Around 50 seconds
  • Strategies: The player should collect the smiley faces to double to shooting speed. Also, when the power gets to 100%, the player should press 'A' button to use the super power.
